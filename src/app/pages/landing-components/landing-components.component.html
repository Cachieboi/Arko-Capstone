About Us

UAPSA stands for United Architects of The Philippines Student Auxiliary- University of Santo Tomas Chapter, one of the duly-recognized student organizations under the UAP. It is affiliated with other UAPSA Chapters and National, with thousands of members from up to 70 different schools all over the Philippines.

The United Architects of the Philippines Student Auxiliary – University of Santo Tomas Chapter (UAPSA-UST), as a recognized student organization, commits to provide the members the architectural knowledge and opportunities essential to their professional and academic achievement guided by the university’s core values – compassion, commitment, and competence – and practice dedication to serving the church and the community.

As an accredited organization of the United Architects of the Philippines, the United Architects of the Philippines Student Auxiliary – University of Santo Tomas (UAPSA-UST) under this Catholic institution envisions to produce globally competitive Thomasian architecture students who execute Thomasian values and equipped with professional and moral formation dedication to service for social transformation.

The organization was established in 2001 by Ar. Marian Eileen Florentino with the sounding goal of producing prime Filipino Architects through loyal camaraderie, honest competition, and a unifying drive to serve.
